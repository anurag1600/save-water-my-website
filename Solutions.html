Research Lab



We propose a state-of-the-art water research lab. In this lab, we will be able to identify toxic substances in water such as:

* Endocrine and DNA disruptors
* Cancer causing agents
* Pesticides
* All harmful chemicals

By identifying these pollutants, we will be able to ensure that present and future water treatment technologies remove these disease-causing chemicals from drinking water.
The lab will be located at Cambridge Innovation Center, Miami. The lab will be able to serve several areas:

* Florida
* The Caribbean
* Central America
* South America










Water Treatment Technology



We have four proprietary technologies that can be used to treat and to clean water. They can be retrofitted to wastewater and drinking water treatment plants in the United States and Canada.

These technologies can be applied to produce drinking water anywhere in the world from any:
* Polluted river
* Polluted lake
* Polluted groundwater

These four technologies can also be used to treat or to recycle wastewater from several industries:
* Food processing
* Pharmaceuticals
* Mining
* Pulp and paper production
* Petrochemicals
* Confined animal feeding operations
* Many other industries
